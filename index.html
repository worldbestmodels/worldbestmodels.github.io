Directed by

World Best Models is Miami's premier modeling agency, founded and led by the talented Leah Kiss — a passionate industry professional dedicated to discovering and developing the next generation of top models.

From mastering the runway walk to booking real campaigns, Leah personally coaches every model through a transformative journey — building confidence, presence, and a career built to last. Based in the heart of Miami, Florida, we work with aspiring and professional models across all experience levels.

Leah's Vision

More Than an Agency —
A Global Mission

Leah Kiss didn't start World Best Models just to run a local agency. Her mission is bigger than Miami — she is here to find raw talent, develop it from the ground up, and put her models in front of the most powerful agencies in the world.

"I train them here in Miami, I take them to Paris, and I put them in front of the people who can change their lives. That's what I do."
— Leah Kiss

From day one, Leah coaches every model personally — posture, walk, confidence, presence. When they are ready, she brings them directly to major open casting calls held by some of the most recognized agencies in the industry. Her models don't just show up to castings — they arrive prepared to win.

And when a model gets signed? Leah doesn't disappear. Once you're signed, she is with you every single step of the way — from your first booking to the biggest runways in the world. Your success is her success, and she treats every model like family.

Good to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Everyone is welcome — all ages and experience levels, from total beginners to working models. There's no strict height requirement to start training; Leah meets you where you are and builds from there. If you're serious about the runway, you're a fit.

WBM is based in Miami, Florida, and most coaching happens in person here. If you're traveling in or based elsewhere, message Leah — she'll let you know what's possible and how castings abroad (like Paris) fit in.

Coaching is offered group or one-on-one, and pricing varies between the two — group sessions are the more affordable way to start, while 1-on-1 is fully personalized. Message Leah for a custom quote based on the format and track you want — she'll walk you through it on a quick call, no pressure.

Leah coaches you until you're ready, then submits you to real open castings held by agencies like New Icon Models and Select. These are genuine, industry-vetted opportunities — not guaranteed bookings. Your job is to be prepared; Leah makes sure you are.

When an agency signs you, they handle your bookings, photoshoots, comp cards, and commission. Leah stays in your corner as your mentor — guiding your career every step of the way, locally and internationally.
