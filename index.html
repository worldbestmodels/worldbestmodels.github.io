01 The Atelier
LK
Plate I — The Directress

Directed by Lea Kiss

World Best Models is Miami's premier modeling agency, founded and led by the talented Lea Kiss — a passionate industry professional dedicated to discovering and developing the next generation of top models.

From mastering the runway walk to booking real campaigns, Lea personally coaches every model through a transformative journey — building confidence, presence, and a career built to last. Based in both Miami and Paris, we work with aspiring and professional models across all experience levels.

Before she was shaping runways, Lea lived them. — Her Story

An international model who walked in Paris and became a muse for the legendary designer Paco Rabanne, she became a Sony Music recording artist — releasing the dance single "Don't You Want Love" and performing across Europe — and later worked with Fashion TV before settling in Miami.

Today she pours those decades of experience — in front of the camera, on the catwalk, and on stage — into the next generation. When Lea coaches you, you're learning from someone who has truly lived the industry.

03 The Passage

More Than an Agency — A Global Mission

Lea Kiss didn't start World Best Models just to run a local agency. Her mission is bigger than Miami — she is here to find raw talent, develop it from the ground up, and put her models in front of the most powerful agencies in the world.

I train them here in Miami, I take them to Paris, and I put them in front of the people who can change their lives. — Lea Kiss

From day one, Lea coaches every model personally — posture, walk, confidence, presence. When they are ready, she brings them directly to major open casting calls held by some of the most recognized agencies in the industry. Her models don't just show up to castings — they arrive prepared to win.

And when a model gets signed? Lea doesn't disappear. Once you're signed, she is with you every single step of the way — from your first booking to the biggest runways in the world. Your success is her success, and she treats every model like family.

07 Notes

Frequently Asked Questions

Everyone is welcome — all ages and experience levels, from total beginners to working models. There's no strict height requirement to start training; Lea meets you where you are and builds from there. If you're serious about the runway, you're a fit.

WBM is based in Miami and Paris, so coaching happens in person in both cities. If you're based somewhere else or traveling in, message Lea — she'll let you know what's possible and how castings between Miami and Paris fit in.

Coaching is offered group or one-on-one, and pricing varies between the two — group sessions are the more affordable way to start, while 1-on-1 is fully personalized. Message Lea for a custom quote based on the format and track you want — she'll walk you through it on a quick call, no pressure.

Lea coaches you until you're ready, then submits you to real open castings held by agencies like New Icon Models and Select. These are genuine, industry-vetted opportunities — not guaranteed bookings. Your job is to be prepared; Lea makes sure you are.

When an agency signs you, they handle your bookings, photoshoots, comp cards, and commission. Lea stays in your corner as your mentor — guiding your career every step of the way, locally and internationally.
